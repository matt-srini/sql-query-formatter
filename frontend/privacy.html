Privacy Policy

🔒 We don’t store your SQL queries

Introduction

SQL Formatter is built to be useful without collecting unnecessary information. We respect user privacy and do not collect personal data as part of the formatting experience.

Data Collection

SQL queries submitted through this website are processed in real time so formatting can be returned immediately.

  • Queries are processed in real time.
  • Queries are not stored.
  • Queries are not logged.
  • Queries are not shared with third parties.

Analytics

This site uses Plausible Analytics to understand overall usage patterns and improve the product. Plausible does not use cookies for this site, and no personal data is collected through analytics.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies.

Third-Party Links

This website may include links to external sites such as GitHub. Those sites operate independently, and their content and privacy practices are not controlled by SQL Formatter.

Contact
